There are three main mailing lists that relate directly to X11 on OS-X.

### X11-Users ###
[x11-users](https://lists.apple.com/mailman3/lists/x11-users.lists.apple.com/) is hosted by lists.apple.com as a forum for users to interact with developers of X11 on OS-X.

### XQuartz-Dev ###
[xquartz-dev](https://lists.macosforge.org/mailman3/lists/xquartz-dev.lists.macosforge.org/) is hosted by lists.macosforge.org as a place for developers to discuss development topics in more detail than would be appropriate for the x11-users list.  Submit patches and discuss development in this list.

### XQuartz-Changes ###
[xquartz-changes](https://lists.macosforge.org/mailman3/lists/xquartz-changes.lists.macosforge.org/) tracks changes to the XQuartz repositories.
